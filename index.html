About Me

Marie Biedermann is a graduate student in the Master's program Game Studies and Engineering. She graduated in English and American Studies (BA 2020) in Graz at the Karl-Franzens University. Her first publication was a paper about an indie game \emph{Gato Roboto} and its moral boundaries and technological invention during war. Soon, a work-in-progress paper about the HEXAD player types in game jam populations will be published by the \emph{CHI PLAY 2023}. On top of that, she participated in the development of eight games, that can be found on her itch.io page (https://vulvarine.itch.io/).
